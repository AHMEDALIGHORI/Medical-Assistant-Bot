# General Health Information

## Regular Checkups

Routine medical checkups help detect conditions early when treatment is most effective. Adults should discuss screening schedules with their healthcare provider based on age family history and risk factors.

## Hand Hygiene

Washing hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds reduces spread of infections. Use alcohol-based sanitizer when soap is not available.

## Balanced Diet

A balanced diet includes vegetables fruits whole grains lean proteins and healthy fats. Limit processed foods added sugars and excessive salt.

## Mental Health

Mental health is as important as physical health. Persistent sadness anxiety or changes in sleep and appetite may warrant speaking with a counselor or doctor.

## When to Seek Care

Consult a healthcare professional for symptoms that are severe sudden unusual or do not improve with home care. This assistant provides general information only and does not replace professional medical advice.
